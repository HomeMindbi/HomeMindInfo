What Is HomeMind?

HomeMind uses AI to document your belongings with just a few photos or a video. It automatically creates a detailed, organized list of your items—perfect for insurance claims.

Who Is It For?

Homeowners and renters who want to protect the value of their belongings. Insurance companies often require more proof than expected—HomeMind ensures you’re ready.

How HomeMind Helps

Quickly catalog your belongings in minutes. HomeMind’s AI identifies items, even in clutter, and records important details like brand names or titles.

Why It Matters

Insurance companies are notorious for trying to reduce payouts. HomeMind is a security measure for you, ensuring that your valuables are accurately recorded before any unforeseen events.
